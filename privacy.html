Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Compliance with GDPR

This policy is built in absolute compliance with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We are deeply committed to respecting user privacy and ensuring that any potential interactions with our web systems remain entirely secure and transparent.

2. Minimization of Data Collection

Luxor explicitly adheres to the principle of data minimization. This website operates strictly as an informational portal. We do not deploy tracking algorithms, user account databases, or analytical systems designed to collect your personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Technical Logs and Hosting

Standard network transmission data (such as temporary IP addresses, browser types, and access timestamps) may be automatically processed by local hosting servers to maintain connection stability. This basic technical data is never cross-referenced, stored long-term, or transferred to third-party commercial entities.

4. User Rights under EU Law

Under the European Union legal framework, you retain the complete right to request the checking, modification, or total erasure of any potential data trace. Since no user data databases exist within our architecture, your standard privacy rights are completely secured by default.
