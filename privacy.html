Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Compliance with GDPR

This policy is built in absolute compliance with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We are deeply committed to respecting user privacy and ensuring that any potential interactions with our web systems and software remain entirely secure and transparent.

2. Minimization of Data Collection (Website)

Luxor explicitly adheres to the principle of data minimization. This website operates strictly as an informational portal. We do not deploy tracking algorithms, user account databases, or analytical systems designed to collect your personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Software Applications and Local Storage (Voxaller)

Our downloadable software applications operate strictly on a local, privacy-first basis:

  • Any configurations, user preferences, or memory assets (such as data.json and memory.json) are stored exclusively on your local device.
  • No local data, user settings, or application interactions are ever uploaded, transmitted, or shared with Luxor or any third-party servers.

4. Technical Logs and Hosting

Standard network transmission data (such as temporary IP addresses, browser types, and access timestamps) may be automatically processed by local hosting servers or standard web tools to maintain connection stability and monitor download traffic. This basic technical data is never cross-referenced, stored long-term, or transferred to third-party commercial entities.

5. User Rights under EU Law

Under the European Union legal framework, you retain the complete right to request the checking, modification, or total erasure of any potential data trace. Since no user data databases exist within our architecture, your standard privacy rights are completely secured by default.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the administration at: luxorcompanyofficial@gmail.com
